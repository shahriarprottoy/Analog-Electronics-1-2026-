# 1N4148 Diode – Explanation

## Forward Voltage

According to the datasheet, the typical forward voltage of the diode is about **0.72 V when the forward current is 10 mA**.

This means that when the diode is conducting current in the forward direction, approximately 0.7 V is required across the diode for current to flow.

## Maximum Reverse Voltage

The maximum reverse voltage (VRRM) is **100 V**.

This means the diode can block up to 100 volts in reverse bias before breakdown may occur.

## Maximum Forward Current

The maximum continuous forward current is **200 mA**.

If the current exceeds this value, the diode may overheat and become damaged.

## Operating Temperature Range

The operating junction temperature range is:

**−65°C to +175°C**

This means the diode can function safely within this temperature range.

## Real Application

A common application of the 1N4148 diode is **signal switching in digital circuits**.

Because this diode switches very quickly, it is used in logic circuits, protection circuits, and high-speed signal processing where fast response time is required.
